13.1 Program Description

Accelerep operates an SMS notification program that sends transactional, workflow-driven text messages to authorized users — specifically sales representatives, managers, and administrators — within your organization's Accelerep workspace. SMS messages are sent exclusively to internal team members; they are never sent to your customers or external contacts.

The types of messages sent under this program include:

Message Type Description
Task & Deadline Reminders Alerts sent when a task is approaching its due date or has become overdue, to help reps stay on top of follow-ups and commitments.
Pipeline & Deal Alerts Notifications when a deal changes stage, stalls, or requires attention — including high-value opportunity alerts for managers.
Daily Digest Summaries A daily summary of open tasks, upcoming deadlines, and pipeline activity delivered to keep reps and managers informed.
Quote Approval Notifications Alerts sent when a quote is submitted for manager approval or when an approval decision has been made on a pending quote.

SMS notifications are an optional feature of the Service. By enabling SMS notifications within your Accelerep account settings and providing a mobile phone number, you expressly consent to receive text messages from Accelerep at the number provided. Consent to receive SMS messages is not a condition of using the Service.

Organization Admins who enable SMS notifications for other users are responsible for ensuring those users have been informed about the messaging program and have provided appropriate consent.

13.3 Opt-Out

To re-enable notifications after opting out via STOP, reply START to any prior message or re-enable the feature in your account settings.
